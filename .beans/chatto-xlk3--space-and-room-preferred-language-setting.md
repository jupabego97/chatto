Allow space and room administrators to configure a preferred language for their space/room.

**Space level:**
- Add preferred language field to space settings
- Display the preferred language on the space info/discovery page

**Room level:**
- Add preferred language field to room settings (requires Room Settings infrastructure)
- Display the preferred language in room header or info panel
- Room can inherit from space or override with its own preference

This helps users understand the expected communication language in a space or room before joining.
